About Me

I was born in Co.Tipperary in 1990. After qualified in nursing I had a car accident when I was 26 yrs. Whihc left me paralysed from neck down.

I always have an interest in art,drawaing and painting but it was in 1990 when I came to live in Dublin that I got the opportunity to participate in a numer of courses in art and computers. I attended evening art classes where I learned to paint with watercolours and oils with a brush in my mouth. I realised my limitations are merely physical,spiritually I'm capable of achieving the things that are important to me and creatively. I'm in the process of achieving them. I completed a 2 year Art link - Art cert course in 1998 and my motivation for learning and knowledge has increased.

I had use of a studio in Temple Bar for 3 years. I paint in Cuan Aoibheann in hte Phoenix Park where I reside. I like painting in OILS, ACRYLICS and WATERCOLOUR. My FAVOURITE SUBJECT is landscapes and I paint my own greeting cards.

I joined the Mouth and Foot Artists association summer 2000. They exhibit my paintings in Liechtestein. I now attend an art group in Palmerstown every week.
